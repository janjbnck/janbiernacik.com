About Me

I come from Poland and therefore speak Polish fluently in addition to German and English. I’ve been fascinated by computers and everything related to them since I was a child. At the age of 12, I discovered my interest in web development and began creating my own small websites.

I have acquired most of my knowledge over the years through self-study. Today, I’m not only proficient in web development but also skilled in Python and use Linux as my desktop operating system.

Through two school internships, I have gained valuable insights and further strengthened both my interest and abilities. Since I’ve always wanted to turn my hobby into a profession, I will begin my apprenticeship as an IT specialist for application development at the Fricke Group in August.
