About Me

I come from Poland so, in addition to German and English, I also speak fluent Polish. Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by computers and everything related to them. At the age of 12, I discovered my interest in web development and started creating small websites.

I have acquired most of my knowledge over the years through self-teaching. Today, I am not only skilled in web development but also proficient in Python and use Linux as my preferred desktop operating system.

Two school internships have given me practical insights and further strengthened my interest and skills. Since I have always wanted to turn my hobby into a career, I will begin my apprenticeship as an IT specialist for application development at the Fricke Group in August.

Outside of Work

In my free time, I enjoy cycling. I particularly like exploring new cities and routes around Hamburg. I'm also a member of a shooting club in Hamburg-Wandsbek, although I haven't participated in training for about two years due to an inconvenient school schedule.
